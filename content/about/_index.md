Rocky Branches, LLC is a scientific consultancy offering professional services across geospatial analysis, infrastructure automation, and scientific computing.

We build and maintain open source tooling for:
- Geospatial information systems (GIS) and spatial analysis
- Road network modeling and optimization
- Cloud and on-premise infrastructure management
- Resource utilization monitoring and analysis
- Developer tooling and automation

Our work spans the full stack — from low-level C++ and embedded tooling to Python-based data pipelines, Kubernetes orchestration, and NixOS infrastructure.
